About Nicole

My name is Tafadzwa Nicole Kurwa, commonly known as Faffy-a nickname whose origin remains a mystery to me. Recently completing my first year of undergraduate studies was a remarkable experience, surpassing my initial expectations. Grateful for reaching this milestone, I can jokingly claim the title of a survivor. Now, let's dive into the "About Nicole" section without further delay.

Initiating an introduction is a bit perplexing, isn't it? I possess a multitude of passions, evident from the content on my homepage. Actively engaging in various pursuits, I proudly embrace the label of a multitasker. The prospect of prolonged idleness seems unfathomable to me; I believe I'd go a bit crazy. While acknowledging the potential perception of sounding like a high-level narcissistic snob, I assure you that this section genuinely reflects who I amâ€”Nicole, in all her uniqueness and humor.

Traveling, as mentioned earlier, is a definite goal for me, likely at the very top of my list. My aspiration is to become a nomadic adventurer, exploring different corners of the world and witnessing its wonders firsthand. American Samoa holds a particular allure for me, not just for its captivating cultural traditions, generous people, and intriguing acquaintances. I am open to exploring any islands, regardless of the location-I am always eager for new adventures. Speaking of adventures, I harbor dreams of experiencing Paris, from climbing the steps of The Arc de Triomphe and appreciating the fine art at the Louvre to catching a glimpse of the Crown of Thorns at Notre Dame and, of course, capturing moments at the iconic Eiffel Tower. After all, who visits Paris and leaves without doing that?

Some might perceive me as reserved, but I prefer to describe myself as an ambivert, fluctuating between introversion and extroversion based on the situation. I consider myself genuinely cheerful, and my internal motto always emphasizes making smiles a priority. I like to see myself as a harbinger of happiness, having discovered contentment through my faith in Christ. As a wholehearted believer in the Almighty God, I hold a vision of the world as one harmonious and joyful family, free from sorrow, suffering, death, or divisive issues like racism. While this utopian world may seem challenging to attain, I remain hopeful. I aspire to contribute to creating a better world for all, and I believe my every kind gesture towards others plays a part in that endeavor. The most fulfilling aspect of my life is knowing that I have concrete ideas to bring about positive change, aiding those in need, striving for equality, and promoting an impartial existence. It's simply a matter of acquiring the necessary resources and actively implementing all that I have envisioned.
