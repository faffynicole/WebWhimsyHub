About Me

My name is Tafadzwa Nicole Kurwa, commonly known as Faffy-a nickname whose origin remains a mystery to me. I originate from Zimbabwe, a country located in Southern Africa. Recently completing my first year of undergraduate studies was a remarkable experience, surpassing my initial expectations. Grateful for reaching this milestone, I can jokingly claim the title of a survivor. Now, let's actually dive into the "About Me" section without further delay.

Initiating an introduction is a bit perplexing, isn't it? I possess a multitude of passions, evident from the brief overview on my homepage. Actively engaging in various pursuits, I proudly embrace the label of a multitasker. The prospect of prolonged idleness seems unfathomable to me; I believe I'd go a bit crazy. While acknowledging the potential perception of sounding like a high-level narcissistic snob, I assure you that this section genuinely reflects who I am-Nicole, in all her uniqueness and humor.

Traveling, as mentioned earlier, is a definite goal for me, likely at the very top of my list. My aspiration is to become a nomadic adventurer, exploring different corners of the world and witnessing its wonders firsthand. American Samoa holds a particular allure for me, not just for its captivating cultural traditions, generous people, and intriguing acquaintances. I am open to exploring any islands, regardless of the location-I am always eager for new adventures. Speaking of adventures, I harbor dreams of experiencing Paris, from climbing the steps of The Arc de Triomphe and appreciating the fine art at the Louvre to catching a glimpse of the Crown of Thorns at Notre Dame and, of course, capturing moments at the iconic Eiffel Tower. After all, who visits Paris and leaves without doing that?

Some might perceive me as reserved, but I prefer to describe myself as an ambivert, fluctuating between introversion and extroversion based on the situation obviously. I consider myself genuinely cheerful, and my internal motto always emphasizes making smiles a priority. I like to see myself as a harbinger of happiness, having discovered contentment through my faith in Christ. As a wholehearted believer in the Almighty God, I hold a vision of the world as one harmonious and joyful family, free from sorrow, suffering, death, or divisive issues like racism. While this utopian world may seem challenging to attain, I remain hopeful. I aspire to contribute to creating a better world for all, and I believe my every kind gesture towards others plays a part in that endeavor. The most fulfilling aspect of my life is knowing that I have concrete ideas to bring about positive change, aiding those in need, striving for equality, and promoting an impartial existence. It's simply a matter of acquiring the necessary resources and actively implementing all that I have envisioned.

During my childhood, when asked about my aspirations, I found myself without a specific answer. It wasn't due to a lack of direction, but rather a desire to explore various possibilities, making it challenging to pinpoint a starting point. Fortunately, that mindset remains unchanged. I aim to embrace every opportunity that comes my way, recognizing that the world offers countless possibilities. I firmly believe that with dedication, anyone can achieve their aspirations. While the world may pose limitations, altering one's perspective can open up new possibilities. Currently, I am pursuing a technical career,

but this is just one component of my broader ambitions, after all, "The world's my oyster", anyone's oyster to be honest.
To reach my current position, I attribute my motivation to internal factors. There has always been an innate drive within me that has prevented me from giving up. Reflecting on my journey and considering my starting point, I can affirmatively state that my current location exceeds my initial expectations. This is indeed fortunate and a great privilege. Contemplating my past, present, and future aspirations now serves as a catalyst for my determination. I can confidently assert that my perseverance has strengthened significantly, and real-life experiences have proven to be invaluable teachers.

Alright, considering this is a personal note, let me share my preferred music genres with y'all. I'm the kind of person who embraces a diverse range of music genres-a true "one size fits all" enthusiast, especially when it comes to music. My taste tends to shift based on my mood at any given moment, without adhering to any particular genre. However, I do have a special fondness for music featuring the Cello instrument, Italian Tango compositions, and Country tunes, with a sprinkle of Jazz. I understand this might contradict my earlier statement, but these genres become my go-to choices when I want to listen to something specific.

I have a fondness for learning in general, and today I'd like to specifically discuss my enthusiasm for acquiring new languages. In 2021, I came across a language learning application called Duolingo, offering a variety of language courses. I decided to enroll in several of them, and the experience has been truly rewarding. Currently, I am engaged in learning Zulu, German, Swahili, and Spanish, with plans to add more languages over time. Learning exotic languages not only provides me with a valuable skill but also serves as a captivating window into different cultures. Words, after all, form the essence of everything. This language-learning journey is not only educational but also enjoyable, and I find great satisfaction in it. Here's a brief introduction in the languages I've acquired:

  • Zulu: Igama lami nguTafadzwa. Ngihlala eFourways.
  • German: Ich bin Tafadzwa und ich bin sehr klug.
  • Spanish: Mi nombre es Tafadzwa y vivo en Fourways.
  • Swahili: Mimi ni Tafadzwa na ninaishi Fourways.
